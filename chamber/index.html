Coyote Creek

News Release

Coyote Creek has been awarded a grant for downtown development. Mayor Tyler Benchmark indicated that Stacy Denmark, the Coyote Creek president of the local Chamber of Commerce applied for the grant last May. "We are very excited for the improvements and opportunities this will bring to our community. We are so pleased that we were awarded these monies." Plans to extend the walking path along Coyote Creek are already in the development stages. A portion of the grant is also slated for use in further improving the Davidson Water Park by adding children's water features as well as making repairs to the deck and bottom of the pool. Residents can look for these improvements starting next summer.
