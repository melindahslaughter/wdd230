Target Audience

The target audience is tourists, locals, and local business owners.

Persona/Tourist

Coyote Creek Wyoming has a strong tourist industry.
Many of the people visiting this site will be tourists
who are usually middle or upper class. They come in the
summer for hiking, camping and fishing, and in the winter
for skiining and snow machining. They will be looking
for events, accomidations and services that have to do with tourism.
They also come to see the rodeo or just to enjoy the vibes of
this beautiful small town at the foot of the Big Horn Mokuntains.

Second Persona/Local

Local residents and local business owners will also visit the site.
They will be looking for ways to promote their business. Demographically,
they range from low income to millionaires. Many of them will visit
the site for date of local events, or to access participation in
local events.

Scenarios

  • Where can I go skiing?
  • Where can I find a hunting or fishing guide?
  • What hiking trails are there and how difficult are they?
  • When is Rodeo Week?
  • When is the Street Fair?
  • When is the Christmas stroll?
  • What restrants are available?
  • What hotels are there?
  • How do I sign up for the Street fair?
  • What is the local weather?
